# Brand Voice — Tempo Flow

Warm, direct, specific, never shaming. Uncle Iroh with a notebook.

## Use

small, gentle, anchor, tempo, nudge, arrive, settle, steady, try, offer, suggest, allow, notice, slow down, take a breath, low-spoons day

## Avoid

crush, smash, hustle, win, beast, dominate, level up, ninja, must, required, mandatory, failed, grind, 10x, optimize, maximize, "You've got this, champ!"

## Tone by surface

- **Onboarding** — calm, curious: "Let's start small. What does today want to be?"
- **Empty state** — gentle: "Nothing here yet. We can start with a brain dump when you're ready."
- **Coach suggestion** — confident but offering: "Three things look doable this afternoon. Want me to stage them?"
- **Error** — honest, brief: "Couldn't reach Google Calendar. Trying again in a minute."
- **Celebration** — warm, understated: "Five days in a row. Nice."
- **Broken streak** — accepting: "You missed yesterday. That's allowed. Back in the chair?"

## Ready-made copy (verbatim from brand doc)

- CTA: **Start your seven-day walk with Tempo Flow — $1.**
- Empty tasks: **Nothing planned yet. Want me to suggest three things based on yesterday?**
- AI accept/reject: **I think I can add this. Accept, tweak, or skip?**
- Missed streak: **You missed yesterday. That's allowed. Small step today?**
- Error: **Couldn't reach Google Calendar. I'll try again in a minute.**
- Trial ending: **Your seven days end tomorrow. Keep going, pause, or walk away — all are fine.**

## Never

Confetti celebrations. Trophies. Streak-saviour drama. Dark patterns. Fake scarcity. "Last chance!" banners. Leaderboards. Military/hustle/war metaphors. Emoji-speak on serious surfaces.
