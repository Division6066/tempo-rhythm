About

A planner, by one person, for other brains.

Tempo Flow was started in the winter of 2024 by Amit Levin, after another productivity app politely shamed him for missing three days of journaling in a row. It has been built slowly, alone, in a small apartment in Tel Aviv.

It is built for ADHD brains, autistic brains, anxious brains, burned-out brains, and anyone who has ever felt that the calendar was a cathedral they could not enter. It does not aspire to be an operating system. It aspires to be a friend who asks, gently, what today could look like.

It is dogfooded before it is shipped. It is patent-held at the workflow layer and otherwise source-available under BUSL-1.1. The commitment is a dollar. The filter is intention.
