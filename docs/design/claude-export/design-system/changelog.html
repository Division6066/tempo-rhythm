Quiet shipping notes.

Apr 23 · v1.0.0 · foundation

Tempo 1.0 — a beginning.

  • All 42 screens live on web PWA, iOS, and Android.
  • $1 seven-day trial via RevenueCat. No auto-renewal, no dark patterns.
  • Coach warmth dial (0 through 10) with a default of 6 — gentle-but-not-saccharine.
  • Onboarding now captures pronouns, one wobble, and one thing you'd like to hold onto this week.
  • First-run brain dump gets sorted into three doable things before the app asks anything else of you.
Apr 14 · v0.9.5 · beta polish

Closed beta polish.

  • Brain Dump sorts by energy on low-spoons days.
  • Fixed a case where the streak banner read too sharp on missed days.
  • Added OpenDyslexic as a single toggle in Preferences.
  • Journal prompts now vary with detected mood — no more "what are you grateful for" at 11pm on a bad Tuesday.
Apr 02 · v0.9.0 · closed beta

Thirty people, four weeks.

  • Thirty users, thirty letters, thirty gentle bug reports.
  • Journal entries now encrypted at rest with per-user keys.
  • First weekly recap generation — the coach writes it, you can edit it.
  • Two-pane layout settled: sidebar + main content. Mobile gets its own set of frames.
Feb 14 · v0.6.0 · first internal build

A founder dogfoods.

  • Core flow complete — dump → coach → plan → do.
  • Amit runs his own day through it for thirty consecutive mornings before telling anyone about it.
  • The "nothing" button makes its first appearance: close the laptop, the day ends well.
